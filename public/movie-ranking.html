The Best Movies According to Ketan Pandey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sooryavansham

Amitabh Bachchan in a dual role portrays a father-son conflict rooted in tradition and ambition.

3 Idiots

A heartwarming comedy-drama that questions the pressure-filled Indian education system.

Veer-Zaara

An epic cross-border love story of sacrifice, hope, and eternal devotion.
